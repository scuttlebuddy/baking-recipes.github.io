Potato Sardine Pies

Ingredients

    Filling:
  • 500g potatoes
  • 2tbspn oil
  • 2 onions, peeled and chopped
  • ½ tbspn curry powder
  • ½ salt
  • 1 small can sardine, deboned and mashed
    • Pastry:
  • 1 portion basic pie pastry

Instructions

  1. Cook potatoes in boiling water until soft. Remove and leave to cool before peeling the skin. Cut into small cubes. Heat up oil and saute onion until fragrant. Add in the remaining ingredients and stir-fry until well-mixed. Add in potatoes and stir well. Dish up and leave to cool.
  2. Prepare 12 egg tart moulds. Roll dough into 4mm thickness. Use round shape cutter(larder than tart mould about 1") to cut out thin, round shape.
  3. Line the pastry onto mould and spoon some filling onto it. Cover with another piece of pastry. pres around the the edge with fork to secure it.
  4. Brush the surface with beaten egg and bake in a preheated oven at 180Â°C for 30-35 minutes until golden brown. Remove and serve.
