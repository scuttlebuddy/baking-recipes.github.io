Mango Cheesecake

Ingredients

Cream Cheese Base(8" round mould):

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 30g brown sugar
  • 15g cocao powder
  • 100g melted unsalted butter

Filling:

  • 200g cream cheese
  • 60g sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 250g Millac cream
  • 100g mango puree
  • 1 tbsp juice and zest of lemon
  • 100g mango cubes
  • 12g gelatin powder + 30g hot water

Mango Mirror:

  • 150g mango puree
  • 1tsp gelatin powder
  • 80g hot water

Instructions

    Biscuit Base:
  1. Mix crushed biscuit and butter evenly into mould and chill for 15 minutes
    Filling:
  1. Cream the cheese and half of sugar until creamy
  2. Egg yolk and falf sugar over double boiler till light and fluffy
  3. Add ② to ① and mix well
  4. Whip up the Millac cream to soft peak
  5. Dissolve the gelatine powder with hot water
  6. Add the gelatine, juice into ③
  7. Lastly add ④ into the mixture
    Mango mirror:
  1. Dissolve gelatine with how water
  2. Pour in mango puree and mix well
